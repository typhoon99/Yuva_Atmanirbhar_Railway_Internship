Background And Vision

Indian Railways started its service 161 years prior on 16 April 1853 when the first passenger train was run over a stretch of 33 kilometres from Mumbai to Thane. Around 13 million people in India commute by railways every day. Being one of the largest organisations globally, Indian Railways demands increasing participation of the youth to carry forward this prestigious legacy of Indian Railways and take the organisation to the zenith of innovation. There has also been a long-standing demand from the Indian Higher Education sector to provide students with opportunities to implement their learnings for the benefit of government organisations and enterprises.

AICTE and Ministry of Railways bring together this state of the art and one of its kind internship platform to mobilise the power of our youth and achieve the vision of “Atmanirbhar Bharat” by our Hon’ Prime Minister Shri. Narendra Modi. With the help of this platform, energetic and skilful youth of our nation from various backgrounds can get to work with the prestigious Indian Railways as interns in a wide range of projects like Intelligent Signalling, Efficient Electrification, Artificial Intelligence Machine Learning,Human Resource Planningand Strategic Management. To provide opportunities even in the remotest part of the country, Indian Railways has ensured that the applicants can choose any zone and division of their choice to work on their projects. In these challenging times of COVID-19 pandemic, the organisation has also provided students with a blend of offline and online projects which can be done at ease from their homes. Not only can the students opt from the various project options, but they can also propose a project topic of their choice, which they think can specifically add value to the Railways ecosystem.To provide a hassle-free registration experience to the students, the portal can be accessed in 8 different Indian languages.

With lakhs of internship opportunities available Pan-India, this internship scheme will prove to be the largest internship scheme globally. The scheme will also be the biggest contributor in achieving the target of 1 crore internship opportunities in India by 2025. Students and youth from all over India are welcome to be a part of this novel internship scheme and experience the zeal of working with the life-line of Indian transport.

Platform Structure

The program is being implemented through a partnership between Ministry of Railways and AICTE, Ministry of Education. At the State level, it will be supported by the Zonal and their respective nodal Officers of the Indian Railways in to adopt this scheme in their respective divisions and workshops. At the project selection and monitoring level, flexibility has been provided in defining the roles, numbers and other parameters in ways that best benefit their contextual needs.

Outcome

Ministry of Railways : Strengthen institutional capacity of the Indian Railways ecosystem and youth engagement towards upgradation initiatives, creating an ecosystem wherein youth can contribute towards building an innovation driven and state of the art transport mechanism in India.

Zones and Divisions : Skilled, high-quality workforce and innovative citizen-centric youth that help them in addressing critical technological challenges via efficient methodologies.

Participating Interns : Enhanced employability through “learning by doing” and opportunity to co-create innovation for the Indian Railways. The interns would get exposure in a large number of areas including but not limited to Electrical Engineering, Electronics Signalling And Telecommunication, Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Commercial And Marketing, Finance Management, Operations, Management and Logistics, Human Resource Management, Materials Management and linguistic inclusion.
